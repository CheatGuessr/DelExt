About DelExt

DelExt is an exploit created by WebCheats. The exploit recreates the behavior of the LTMEAT Print method by flooding iframes and then printing the page. For whatever reason, printing a page with a ton of iframes hangs the embedded page rather than the page who embedded it. This is exactly what DelExt exploits, since it also works with extension pages (as long as they are under web_accessible_resources). This seems to be much more consistent than other extension-freezing methods, and it also lasts for a longer period of time.
